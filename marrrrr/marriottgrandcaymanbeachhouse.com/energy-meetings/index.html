    Energy meetings

    Plan an inspired event

    At our Beach House, we believe in a different way of doing business. Corporate meetings and events no longer have to be such formal occasions. Here in Cayman we are all about enjoying the island’s endless summer. Our appreciation for the laid-back local culture is even reflected in how we run our events.

    Our aim is to be true innovators of Cayman’s hospitality industry by positioning our events with a comprehensive program that really understands the needs of a new generation of meeting guests who have modern health and holistic needs.

    We believe in an abundance of all that is authentic. We want to be the company that gets you. Work meetings can be stressful. Many traditional business events focus on efficiency and productivity but nothing much beyond that. We want to bring back the fun.

    With this in mind, we have come up with an all-embracing program that connects health, body and mind. This includes a healthy culinary offering with local and organic food options. Our Chefs can even build special menus around different types of “superfoods” ad explain the benefits. We provide our signature ‘Mental Break Boxes’ that include aromatherapy oils, literature on wellness and mandala de-stressing kits to aid in relaxation. We also incorporate natural elements in our décor for the meetings such as terrariums. We love their restorative and calming effects, which encourage renewed energy and wellbeing. We even partner with a local nutritionist or yoga teacher who can share their knowledge and give advice on how to live a healthier lifestyle.
