    a beautiful experience

    Luckily for us at our Beach House, we get to take in this beautiful experience every night at our oceanfront restaurant, Veranda. We are so proud of our recently revamped space, which includes vintage-inspired décor and an air of bohemian chic – there is really no place like it in Cayman.

    There are only a handful places along Seven Mile where you can enjoy a unique culinary experience directly on the beach with direct views of the gorgeous Caribbean Sea from your table.

    FRESH FISH, SIMPLY DONE

    Besides the breathtaking setting, both locals and international visitors alike come to Veranda for our innovative Food and Beverage offering. With dishes like our Cayman Style Bouillabaisse and Caribbean Fish & Chips – we live up to our reputation of offering fresh fish, simply done. Other signature staples on the Veranda menu are Potato Gnocchi, Jerk Cornish Hen and Seven Mile Chocolate Cake (featuring seven layers of caramelized white chocolate ganache!)

    Our Signature Lobster Fete

    One of the best nights to visit Veranda is on Wednesday for our signature Lobster Fete. Our versatile menu offers an all-you-can-eat option as well as ordering a la carte. Delicious dishes include the Grilled Caribbean Lobster, Lobster Mac ‘N’ Cheese and Bistro Steak. We are proud to feature well-known acoustic duo Hi-Tide. Their live performance, which includes classic covers and original island-inspired music, adds another magical element to the evening. Parents are also invited to bring their children to the Kids Club Pajama Party taking place just a stone’s throw away from Veranda. Children can play games, do crafts and watch a movie while parents have a romantic night out.
