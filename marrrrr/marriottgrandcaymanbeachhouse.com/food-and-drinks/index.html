    Food For Thought

    With so many cultures represented in one place, Cayman is fast becoming the cosmopolitan capital of the Caribbean. More than 106 different nationalities can be found, bringing with them various customs and practices from every corner of the globe. This is most apparent in the diversity of our island’s culinary offering. There is a mixture of gastronomic influences stemming from South America, the Caribbean, Europe and beyond.

    We are very proud of our Food & Beverage program at our Beach House. At our restaurants, we provide fresh, authentic and innovative dishes. We travel the world to find inspiration for our gastronomic offering – from Barcelona for our El Mercat Tapas Night to London for our Mad Tea Party, we love discovering new and exciting ideas to take back to Cayman to make our own. What we offer guests is a unique multi-sensory culinary experience, inspired by our island’s international flair.

    The Farm-To-Table Movement in Cayman

    Our Beach House is proud to support the thriving Farm-To-Table movement in Cayman. Where we can in our kitchen, we choose local and sustainably grown produce. We have close relationships with a number of farmers based in the East End of the island and our chefs frequently visit them to handpick the fruits, vegetables and other items that will be the ingredients of many of our innovative dishes. Our Food & Beverage program changes with the seasons and we use local food to inspire many of the dishes and the cocktails that our guests enjoy. We love to eat local in Cayman!
