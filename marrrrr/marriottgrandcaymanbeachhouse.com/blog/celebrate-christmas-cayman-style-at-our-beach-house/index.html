    CELEBRATE CHRISTMAS CAYMAN-STYLE AT OUR BEACH HOUSE

    Christmas is nearly upon us and we are getting ready for all the merry-making of the holiday season. We have so much to look forward to at our Beach House!
     
    We are dreaming of a white (sand!) Christmas this year and the festivities commence on Monday, 22 December when we kick off a jam-packed holiday program of fun Kids Club and recreation activities for all ages including Santa Hat Decorating, Sunrise Yoga, Teen Paddleboard Races and a Bubble Wand Magic Show at the beach (just to name a few!)
     
    On Wednesday, 24 December there will be a number of Christmas-themed activities, with our version of Santa (Caribbean-style) making an appearance on our beach from 4pm and a special Christmas Eve Beach Bonfire with S’mores starting at 7pm. We will also have Christmas Caroling at 8pm and the Sky Lantern Ceremony at 9pm. It is such a magical way to spend Christmas Eve with family, friends and loved ones! Our oceanfront restaurant, Veranda, will also be hosting a Christmas Eve Special Dinner, which includes a Raw Bar, Risotto Action Station and Turkey & Prime Rib Carving Station, as well as our decadent Dessert Bar.
     
    On Thursday, 25 December the Christmas cheer continues with a Caribbean Sand Man Building Competition on the beach at 7am, Beach Sand Globes Making at 11am, Christmas Cookie Decorating at 1pm and a one-of-a-kind Christmas Brunch at Anchor & Den from 12–3pm. Our Boulangerie Brunch is the perfect occasion to gather and celebrate together on Christmas Day. The evening concludes with a Poolside Christmas Movie at 7pm. Watch a classic Christmas film on the giant screen under the stars – we will be handing out free popcorn for all.
     
    Get in the holiday spirit and join us at our Beach House for an unforgettable Cayman-Style Christmas!
     
    For some fun facts on Christmas traditions in Cayman, check out the Cayman Islands Tourism website. Happy Holidays!
