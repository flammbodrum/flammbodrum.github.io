    The Gemini New Moon: Follow Your Curiosity

    Exclusively written for the Beach House by Lennon Mara of Moon + Quartz

    Can you feel that change in the air? With the Sun newly arrived in Gemini, there’s a brand new brightness to the energy out there. And with a New Moon in Gemini just around the corner on Thursday, May 25th, we’ve got an amazing opportunity to set intentions that have the power to change the way we think and perceive the world around us.
                                                                           
    Gemini energy is capable of seeing facts as disparate ideas, flying from one to another to another with little weight or meaning distinguishing one from the next. While this might become problematic for pursuits staying a bit more power, the tendency is a true gift, allowing those attuned to Gemini energy to follow wherever their curiosity leads, sampling and picking up morsels of insight and information that can amass into something magnificent and new further down the road.
                                                               
    This New Moon is a time to tap into that childlike Gemini curiosity. What piques your attention? What’s crossed your path lately that’s provoked questions in you? What are you yearning to explore? What haven’t you been able to stop thinking about?
     
    More than anything else, this time is about opening up this space within and saying yes to that curiosity. On the most fundamental level, we create our experience of being in the world through what we’re able to perceive. Now is an opportunity to open up and explore after the end of a long, withdrawn winter — and to use whatever you discover to enhance the way you see everything around you.

    If travel has lit the fires of curiosity within you, the Dark Night event in Anchor & Den at Marriott’s Beach House in Cayman on May 26th might be the perfect destination for setting your New Moon intentions. Lit candles, burning sage, deep tunes, intentional community and expansive ocean views will help you to let go and follow where your heart leads.
