    THE BEACH HOUSE EXPERIENCE

    We are so excited about our recent metamorphosis – it can be experienced everywhere from our modern décor and furnishings to our innovative Food & Beverage offering. Even our Beach House Experience insignia has gotten a makeover! We feel that it represents our fresh new look and approach. We hope you like it!
     
    You may have heard us refer to the Beach House Experience before, but what is it all about? Good question. Here in Cayman, we are all about enjoying the island’s endless summer. Our aim is to evoke the happy nostalgia of summer – a time when life is bright and carefree. We do our best to provide guests with the ultimate Beach House Experience by creating a fun and relaxed atmosphere where family and friends can reunite, reconnect and spend quality time together.
     
    We offer a variety of summertime activities like making S’mores at our bonfire nights on the beach, watching poolside movies under the stars, stargazing with a knowledgeable guide, as well as handing out summertime treats like cotton candy and popcorn on a hot day by the pool and beach.
     
    We provide guests with Linus bike rentals (included in the Resort Fee) so that they have the chance to explore our beautiful island like a local. We have a daily ritual where we set up colorful furniture right along our beachfront at the end of the day so guests have an ideal vantage point to take the perfect photo as the sun goes down. It is also a fun way to have your picture taken with the gorgeous Cayman sunset as your backdrop.
     
    In a nutshell, the Beach House Experience is all about bringing you back to that summer state of mind. Because it isn’t just a season – summer for us is a way of life!
