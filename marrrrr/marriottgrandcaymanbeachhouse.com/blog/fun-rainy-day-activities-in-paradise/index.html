    FUN RAINY DAY ACTIVITIES IN PARADISE

    Being in Cayman means we are blessed with glorious sunshine throughout the year, but even paradise has its rainy days. Here are five fun ideas on what to do on a wet day like today!

    Curl up and read a book. Do you remember the last time that you were able to do this? We live in a world full of distractions – our smart phones and iPads have become extensions of us. Rainy days are a great opportunity to disconnect from technology and indulge in a good ‘old-fashioned’ book!

    Get your dose of culture. Check out the National Gallery of the Cayman Islands to view beautiful pieces by local artists as well as artists from neighboring Caribbean islands. Want to learn more about Cayman history and heritage? Look no further than the Cayman Islands National Museum, which houses the island’s national treasures. 

    Cuddle up and watch a movie. Check out the cinema in Camana Bay for the latest blockbusters. This is a great activity for families and couples alike!

    Play board games. Trivial Pursuit, Scrabble, Monopoly – what is your favorite game? Take time to reconnect with friends and family over a classic board game. You can even take the opportunity to enjoy a cup of hot chocolate while you play (it is not everyday that you can say that you drank hot chocolate in the Caribbean!) Don’t forget to check out our Games Zone as well on the third floor of the resort, which features an air hockey table plus so much more.

    Pamper yourself with a spa day. At our Beach House, you can spoil yourself with a luxurious treatment at Le Mer Spa. Have a massage, a facial or get your hair done. You don’t need a rainy day to indulge in some TLC.
