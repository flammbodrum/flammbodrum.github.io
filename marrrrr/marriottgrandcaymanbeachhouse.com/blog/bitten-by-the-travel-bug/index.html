    Bitten By The Travel Bug

    Wanderlust: (n.) A strong desire our urge to wonder or travel and explore the world.
     
    At our Beach House, we have wanderlust in our veins. We are nomads at heart – dreaming of faraway places, seeking adventures in all corners of the globe and craving new experiences. We love travelling the world to find inspiration. From our diverse Food & Beverage offering to our eclectic décor, our passion for travel is reflected in everything we do.
     
    Here we share our travel quotes to inspire Wanderlust. Get out there and explore the world!
     
    “For once you have tasted flight you will walk the earth with your eyes turned skywards, for there you have been and there you will long to return.” – Leonardo da Vinci
     
    “Travel brings power and love back to your life.” – Rumi
     
    “My life is shaped by the urgent need to wander and observe, and my camera is my passport.”
     
    “We wander for distraction, but we travel for fulfillment.” – Hilaire Belloc
     
    “To move, to breathe, to fly, to float; to gain all while you give; to roam the roads of lands remote; to travel is to live.” – Hans Christian Andersen
     
    “The world is a book and those who do not travel read only one page.” – St. Augustine
     
    “We travel, some of us forever, to seek other states, other lives, other souls.” – Anais Nin
     
    “Travel makes one modest. You see what a tiny place you occupy in the world.” – Gustave Flaubert
     
    “We are torn between nostalgia for the familiar and an urge for the foreign and strange. As often as not, we are homesick most for the places we have never known.” – Carson McCullers
     
    “My destination is no longer a place, but a new way of seeing.” – Marcel Proust
     
    “Twenty years from now you will be more disappointed by the things you didn’t do than by the ones you did. So throw off the bowlines, sail away from the safe harbor. Catch the trade winds in your sails. Explore. Dream. Discover.” – Mark Twain
