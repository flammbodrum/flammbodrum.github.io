    Our Beach House Unveils Signature Scent

    The new scent program at our Beach House is aromatherapy for the soul. We have partnered with Sydney Hale, a boutique home fragrance brand based in the US, to create the perfect signature scent. A beautiful mixture of bitter orange, driftwood and patchouli has taken over our favourite spots at the resort. We believe that scent is such an integral part of making and remembering special moments that we wanted to create a special scent that would transport our guests to happy times.
     
    There is no doubt that particular smells trigger strong emotions and memories. Here are five fun facts about our fascinating sense of smell.
     
    One of the most evocative smells from childhood is crayons. A survey found that 85% of all people remembered their childhood when they caught the smell of Crayola crayons and the newer crayon-scented coloured pens.
    The sense of smell is the first of all our senses to develop. Even before we are born, our sense of smell is fully formed and functioning.
     
    The smell of a new leather jacket or pair of shoes makes everyone happy. But a new car smells best of all. An artificial “new car smell” is sprayed inside cars that lasts for six weeks.
     
    Your sense of smell accounts for 75-95% of the impact a flavour has. Without being able to smell the difference between onion and potato, it’d be difficult to tell them apart.
     
    Your nose contains 10 million smell receptors, which can distinguish 10 trillion distinct scents!
                                                                                                                            
    We hope that you pop by our Beach House soon to take in our new signature scent!
