    Celebrating Easter At Our Beach House

    Camping over the Easter weekend is a much-loved tradition in Cayman. Hundreds of families and friends make their pilgrimage to the coastline, bringing with them camping equipment and provisions to last them for days. It is one of the only times during the year when loved ones can gather and spend quality time together without the distractions of everyday life.

    Days and nights are often spent barbequing on the beach, playing games along the shoreline and in the water and reminiscing about good times. At our Beach House, we love this time of year because it brings people together to reconnect and collect memories. We have a number of fun activities organized for the long Easter weekend that embraces this wonderful Caymanian tradition. 

    The Easter festivities kick off on Friday, 30 March with the A&D Gin Experience, taking place from 7pm–10pm. There will be a special Pop-Up Gin Bar where we will be sharing our love for this classic drink with our very own A&D Gin. We will be offering guests our Twisted & Bitter cocktail as well as our A&D Gin with a selection of Fever Tree Tonics, plus specials on food. 

    For our in-house guests we will also be offering two Cocktail Master Classes, led by our talented mixologist Luca, taking place on Wednesday, the 28th of March and Wednesday, 4th of April from 3-4:30pm.

    On Sunday, 1 April we will be hosting a special Easter edition of our signature Boulangerie Brunch from 12–3pm. The delicious spread being offered will include a live sashimi station with hamachi, tuna and stone crab as well as fresh wasabi. We will also be featuring a mouthwatering cheese raclette station, authentic Spanish tapas and charcuterie boards, rack of lamb and glazed ham at the Carving Station and an Easter pop-up cocktail station (this is just a glimpse of what is on offer!) We are also excited to be showcasing a special farm inspired chocolate and dessert installation with chicks and Easter eggs. 

    In addition, the fabulous Kids Club team will be hosting its Easter Party for children ages 5–12. Kids will enjoy crafts and games, a special guest appearance by the Easter Bunny and an Easter egg hunt in the Courtyard from 3pm.

    In the evening, we will be hosting a special edition of our beach bonfire and complimentary S’mores from 7pm. 

    On Monday, 2 April we will conclude the long weekend with another signature Boulangerie Brunch, for those that could not make it to our Easter Brunch (or just want to continue the fun!) We will also be encouraging our guests to participate in our Glow Volleyball tournament and other fun games on our beach from 7pm.

    Enjoy some blissful moments on the beach with loved ones this Easter weekend and take part in some of our exciting activities!
