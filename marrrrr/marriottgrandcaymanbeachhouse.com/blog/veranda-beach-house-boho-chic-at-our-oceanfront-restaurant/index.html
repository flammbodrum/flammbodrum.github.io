    VERANDA: BEACH HOUSE BOHO-CHIC AT OUR OCEANFRONT RESTAURANT

    You can ask anyone – part of the ultimate Beach House experience is enjoying a candle-lit dinner on the beach as the sun goes down. The ambience is priceless. The sound of crashing waves in the distance, the feel of the soft warm breeze caressing your skin, the perfect view of the sparkling turquoise ocean as the sun sets on the horizon while the wide-open sky reveals picture-perfect pastels painted across the closing dusk. It looks and feels too good to be true.

    Luckily for us at our Beach House, we get to take in this beautiful experience every night at our oceanfront restaurant, Veranda. We are so proud of this gorgeous space, which includes vintage-inspired décor and an air of bohemian chic – there is really no place like it in Cayman. There are only a handful of places along Seven Mile where you can enjoy a unique culinary experience with direct views of the gorgeous Caribbean Sea from your table.

    One of the best evenings to visit Veranda is on Fridays for Asado, our take on the ultimate steak night experience. Inspired by the Argentinean tradition of cooking with fire, guests are welcome to indulge in this unique foodie experience with family and friends. At the heart of their mouthwatering menu lies grilled meat from the asado (barbeque), which makes up a traditional meal in many households across Argentina. Flavourful dishes include Short Rib Asador, Beef Tenderloin, Hangersteak and Choripan – exquisitely barbecued to perfection and served with homemade sauces. Our offering also features other delicious Argentinean specialties such as Empanadas de Humitas and Provoleta.

    A special Bourbon-inspired mixology menu includes six delicious cocktails, such as their signature Bourbon Spritzer (made with orange zest infused Makers Mark, aperol, crème de casis and Prosecco) that are the perfect accompaniment to our steak dishes.

    Last but not least, parents are also invited to bring their children to the Kids Club Art Shack during Asado. Children can play games, do crafts and watch a movie while parents have a romantic night out (this offering is exclusive to families dining at Asado).

    Visit us at Veranda soon – it is nourishing for the appetite and the soul!

    Veranda is open for lunch from 11:30am–2:30pm and dinner from 5:30–9:30pm.
