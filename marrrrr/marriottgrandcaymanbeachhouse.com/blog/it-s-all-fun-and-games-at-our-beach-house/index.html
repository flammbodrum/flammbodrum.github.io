    It’s All Fun and Games At Our Beach House

    One of our favourite things to do while we relax in paradise is sit with a refreshing drink and play some fun old-school games with friends. It brings us back to a simpler time, when life was slow-paced and technology wasn’t occupying our headspace.
     
    At our Beach House, we like to hand out traditional games like puzzles, playing cards, dominos and board games to guests as they enjoy the company of loved ones. Call us old-fashioned and nostalgic but we can’t think of anything better than basking in the Cayman sunshine, catching up with the people we care about while playing a fun traditional game.
     
    It doesn’t seem like something many families and friends do together anymore, whereas in the past many evenings were spent huddled around a game for hours, drinking and eating, as well as sharing meaningful conversations and funny moments.
     
    Do something different the next time you are at the beach and try your hand at a traditional game like our rustic wooden tic-tac-toe set, which is available at Vista Bar for our guests to enjoy. It is a blast from the past!
     
    A tip from the Beach House experts when playing tic-tac-toe – the center may be the obvious place to begin the game, but next time consider a corner start!
