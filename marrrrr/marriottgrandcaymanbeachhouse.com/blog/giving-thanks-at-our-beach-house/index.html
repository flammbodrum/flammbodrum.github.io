    Giving Thanks At Our Beach House

    At our Beach House, we like to treat everyday like Thanksgiving. We believe that gratitude is one of the most powerful emotions we can practice in our daily lives. Living in paradise, we have so much to be grateful for – the natural beauty that surrounds us, special memories shared with family and friends, plus we love embracing joy in everyday simple moments.
     
    With it being American Thanksgiving, we wanted to share with you our favourite quotes on gratitude that we hope will put into perspective the importance of being thankful not just today, but as often as possible.
     
    Happy Thanksgiving from our Beach House!
     
    “The essence of all beautiful art, all great art, is gratitude.” – Friedrich Nietzsche
     
    “Gratitude can transform common days into thanksgivings, turn routine jobs into joy, and change ordinary opportunities into blessings.” – William Arthur Ward

    "Be thankful for what you have; you'll end up having more. If you concentrate on what you don't have, you will never, ever have enough." – Oprah Winfrey
     
    “Joy is the simplest form of gratitude.” – Karl Barth
     
    “Sometimes we should express our gratitude for the small and simple things like the scent of the rain, the taste of your favorite food, or the sound of a loved one's voice.” – Joseph B. Wirthlin

    “Gratitude is the sign of noble souls.” – Aesop
     
    “Gratitude bestows reverence, allowing us to encounter everyday epiphanies, those transcendent moments of awe that change forever how we experience life and the world.” – John Milton
     
    "The roots of all goodness lie in the soil of appreciation for goodness.” – Dalai Lama
     
    “Begin each day with a grateful heart.” – Unknown

    “Acknowledging the good that you already have in your life is the foundation for all abundance.” – Eckhart Tolle
