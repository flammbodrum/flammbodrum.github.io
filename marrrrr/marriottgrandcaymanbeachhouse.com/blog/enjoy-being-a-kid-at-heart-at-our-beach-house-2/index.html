    ENJOY BEING A KID AT HEART AT OUR BEACH HOUSE

    "It is a happy talent to know how to play." - Ralph Waldo Emerson
     
    "Hold the hand of the child that lives in your soul. For this child, nothing is impossible." - Paulo Coelho

    At our Beach House, we offer a fantastic Kids Club for our junior guests ages five to 12. Our popular program, led by the famous Kathryn and her fun-loving team, is jam-packed with activities that have been inspired by our love of nature and reconnection with family and loved ones. And with the beach as our backyard, there is no shortage of fun in the sun and sand!
     
    Activities include ‘Dolphins & Me,’ an interactive learning session with staff from Dolphin Discovery, ‘Pottery Painting,’ where kids can choose to paint from a variety of ceramics that reflect island life and ‘Come Fly With Me,’ where families can decorate a kite or windsock and fly it together on the beach.
     
    The aim of our Kids Club program is to be less focused on technology and more about the great outdoors with an appreciation of island living. For instance, we offer sessions called ‘Las Tortugas’ where families are invited to learn about turtles as they feed them in our Turtle Lagoon as well as playing a turtle game in our Courtyard. Likewise, ‘Lizard Lovers’ is all about discovering iguanas and lizards.
     
    For older kids, we also host a weekly Family Game Night on the beach with giant games. For the ‘kid’ in all of us, we were inspired by fond childhood memories like stargazing on the beach, movie screenings under the stars (featuring family movies and beach bonfires – including S’mores! At our Beach House, there is definitely something for everyone!
     
    *Please note that Kids Club activities change seasonally.
