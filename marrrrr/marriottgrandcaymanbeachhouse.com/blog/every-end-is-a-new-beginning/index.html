    EVERY END IS A NEW BEGINNING

    There has been a lot happening at our Beach House over the last few weeks. Our resort has undergone a major facelift in September and this week we will be opening our doors to guests to show off our new look and design. We won’t be unveiling our lobby area until November (don’t worry, we are worth the wait!), but in the meantime guests will be able to enjoy all our Beach House amenities, including our newly renovated oceanfront restaurant and bar – now called Veranda and Vista, respectively.
     
    To commemorate all the exciting changes taking place, we thought we would share with you some inspirational quotes on starting afresh. Here’s to new beginnings!
     
    Watch this space and follow us on Facebook to hear all about the changes taking place at our Beach House.
     
    “Every new beginning comes from some other beginning’s end.” – Seneca

    “For last year’s words belong to last year’s language and next year’s words await another voice. And to make an end is to make a beginning.” – T.S. Eliot

    “Every day is a new beginning. Treat it that way. Stay away from what might have been, and look at what can be.” – Marsha Petrie Sue

    “The beginning is the most important part of the work.” – Plato

    “We must always change, renew, rejuvenate ourselves, otherwise we harden.” – Johann Wolfgang von Goethe
