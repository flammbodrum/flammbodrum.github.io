    Dark Night At Our Beach House

    A New Moon represents new beginnings and it is during this time that the moon is empty, receptive and full of potential. It is the optimum time to look deep within and discover your hopes and dreams. 
     
    Setting intentions during a New Moon is a powerful and meaningful way to align with the world around us and provide guidance in our lives. New Moon ceremonies are about creation, manifestation, birth and rebirth while the full moon represents the end of a chapter, tying up loose ends and shedding light on the things that we no longer have to hold onto.
     
    At our Beach House, we believe that celebrating the New Moon is a spiritual way to connect with one’s self, friends and loved ones as well as the universe.
     
    During the New Moon every month, we will be creating a sacred space and dimming the lights in Anchor & Den for an evening of candlelight rituals, vibrating tunes and smoking white sage to honor the celestial cycles of our planet. We also believe that sharing a sacred space will help magnify the power of our intentions.
     
    We welcome you to join us for the unveiling of this original signature event, Dark Night, and enjoy an evening of Apothecary elixirs, holistic bites, stargazing and intention setting. Starting Friday, 6 May 2016 from sunset at Anchor & Den. Follow our Facebook page to be kept up to date on all future events.
