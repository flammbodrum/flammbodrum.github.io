    Summer State of Mind At Our Beach House

    At our Beach House, all we need is a good dose of vitamin sea! For us the ocean soothes and calms our soul. We are enjoying the balmy days of summer and can’t think of a better way to pay homage to our beautiful island than with some inspirational sayings about the sea and paradise living. Here we share a few of our favourite quotes that make us want to stay on the beach forever. 

    “In one drop of water are found all the secrets of all the oceans.” – Kahlil Gibran

    “The ocean stirs the heart, inspires the imagination and brings eternal joy to the soul.” – Robert Wyland

    “The sea, once it casts its spell, hold one in its net of wonder forever.” – Jacques Cousteau

    “To myself I am only a child playing on the beach, while vast oceans of truth lie undiscovered before me.” – Isaac Newton

    “Smell the sea, and feel the sky. Let your soul and spirit fly.” – Van Morrison

    “Paradise isn’t a place. It is a feeling.” – L. Boyer

    “Paradise has never been about places. It exists in moments. In connection. In flashes across time.” – Victoria Erickson

    “Love is a portion of the soul itself, and it is of the same nature as the celestial breathing of the atmosphere of paradise.” – Victor Hugo

    "At the beach, life is different. Time doesn't move hour to hour but mood to moment. We live by the currents, plan by the tides and follow the sun." – Unknown

    “The cure for anything is salt water: tears, sweat or the sea.” – Isak Dinesen

    Here in Cayman, we are all about enjoying the island’s endless summer. Our aim is to evoke the happy nostalgia of summer – a time when life is bright and carefree. We love providing our guests with the ultimate Beach House experience by creating a fun and relaxing atmosphere where family and friends can reunite, reconnect and spend quality time together. We are all about bringing you back to that summer state of mind. Life is better at the beach!
