    Dreaming In Colours Inspired By The Sea

    At our Beach House, we love dancing to the song of the ocean. We count our lucky stars to be living in paradise. The sea provides inspiration to our daily rituals and reminds us to be grateful for the natural abundance that surrounds us.

    We feel so blessed to know that the beach is never too far away and if we wanted to, we can take a dip in the ocean at a moment’s notice. We even dream in colours inspired by the sea!

    Here we share our five favourite quotes inspired by the ocean that will make you want to jump right in:

    “The ocean stirs the heart, inspires the imagination and brings eternal joy to the soul.” – Robert Wyland

    “Live in the sunshine, swim in the sea, drink the wild air.” – Ralph Waldo Emerson

    “You are not a drop in the ocean, you are the entire ocean in a drop.” – Rumi

    “Dance with the waves, move with the sea. Let the rhythm of the water set your soul free.” – Christy Ann Martine

    “The sea, once it casts its spells, holds one in its net of wonder forever.” – Jacques Cousteau
