    The Ultimate Staycation At Our Beach House

    Sparkling turquoise seas. Soft powdery white sand. Beautiful bright sunshine. Warm tropical breezes. These magical ingredients make up the recipe for the perfect summer vacation.
     
    At our Beach House, we are all about the staycation. We feel so blessed to be living in one of the most popular holiday destinations in the Caribbean and with a backyard like Seven Mile Beach, you don’t have to venture far to find the ideal setting to get away from the stress of everyday life.
     
    Here are the top five things that we like to do on a staycation. There is no need to leave the island in search of paradise! 

    Discover some of Cayman’s many fantastic restaurants. There are an abundance to choose from! At our Beach House, we are proud to host Specialty Nights at Anchor & Den, including our popular tapas night, El Mercat – Nit de Tapes on Thursdays. Our Chef de Cuisine, Thais Rodriguez, visited Barcelona this year to discover Spain’s gastronomic delights. Her travels inspired the enticing dishes that we offer; it is fast becoming a favorite night amongst the locals.
     
    Indulge in Sunday brunch. Our signature Boulangerie Brunch is an institution on the island. Besides the buzzing atmosphere and beautiful ambience, our brunch is the ultimate culinary experience, with a diversity of delicious dishes that are internationally inspired. Paired with bottomless glasses of De Chanceny’s Cremant de Loire, it is the perfect combination.
     
    Take a Linus bike and explore the island. You can’t go wrong with a picnic lunch, blanket and a loved one! Get off the beaten path and discover Cayman as a local. Visit a place that you have never been before.
     
    Get in the water. Have you always wanted to get your diving certification but never had the time? Or have you wanted to go snorkeling at other beaches on the east end of the island? A staycation is the perfect opportunity to do a diving course, check out popular diving and snorkeling sites like the Kittiwake and Stingray City during the slower tourist months or take a day trip across the island.
     
    Just sit on the beach and take in the beauty that surrounds you. Even living the dream in Cayman, it is so easy to get distracted with the busyness of life. Get lost in the sound of the crashing waves. Get a tan. Read a book. Listen to inspiring music. Or take the opportunity to sit in a beach chair and just do nothing. Beautiful.
     
    So if you are itching for a holiday but don’t want to venture beyond our beautiful shores, you can find your ultimate retreat at our Beach House. For more information on our staycation rates and offers, click here.
