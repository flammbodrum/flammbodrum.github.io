    ANCHOR & DEN IS GOOD TO-GO!

    At our Beach House, we know our guests love spending time out and about on the beach or exploring the beautiful island. That is why Anchor & Den offers a convenient To-Go option so you can maximise your time out in the sunshine.

    Imagine waking up and enjoying a cup of coffee while sitting on the beach and staring into the sparkling turquoise ocean. The Anchor Lounge has a convenient Grab & Go area featuring delicious “Coffee House Style” premium coffee, freshly-crafted juices made to order (until 11am), sweet and savory crepes from our Creperie Station and homemade pastries and breads that are baked from scratch by our Pastry Chef Lauren and her team everyday. Starting your day off right has never been easier!

    Guests who want to indulge in a take-away option for lunch or dinner have the luxury of choosing from our all-day Anchor & Den menu. Fast snacks like Cheddar Thyme Cornbread with Local Pepper Jelly and Jerk Pork Poutine or more hearty meals like White Truffle Grilled Cheese and Braised Short Rib Gnocchi can all be freshly made to order.

    You can even complete the ultimate summer experience by taking a picnic with you filled with delicious dishes from Anchor & Den. Discover a perfect spot at a nearby beach or park to lay down a blanket and share a picnic with friends and family. You are welcome to take one of our Linus bikes, included in the Resort Fee, to explore the island like a local. We would be more than happy to organise a picnic basket for you to take on your journey. We can personalise it as much as you want – all you have to do is tell us what you would like to be included in your basket!

    And there is no better way to cool down after a sun-filled day at the beach than with a scoop (or two!) of gelato from our Gelato Stand (available from 2pm Monday-Saturdays and 4pm on Sundays). We have partnered up with Gelato & Co. Cremeria Italiana, who make their gelato fresh daily with the highest quality ingredients. With mouthwatering flavours like Bacio – a delicious blend of dark chocolate and hazelnut as well as local mango to name a few; it is a sweet ending to a perfect summer’s day!
