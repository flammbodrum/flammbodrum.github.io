    The Beach House Mercantile – A Retail Space Like No Other

    For the last few weeks, we haven’t been able to contain our excitement about our latest offering, the Beach House Mercantile, a new retail space that features ‘endless summer goods,’ inspired by the fun and free-spirited lifestyle embraced in the Cayman Islands.

    We had so much fun discovering unique items that we found during our travels around the world that we wanted to bring our favourites back to Cayman. What we offer is a curated selection of endless summer goods that we hope will inspire and grab the attention of our guests. We are also collaborating with local companies to feature exciting items that resonate with our unique philosophy and style.

    Some of the cool brands that we carry that have been flying off the shelves are our bags and accessories from Uashmama, our ‘Grand Cayman’ and ‘Endless Summer’ canvas bags made exclusively for us by Forestbound, funky necklaces from Brooklyn Beach Rocks, botanically-inspired products from Naturopathica, beach accessories from The Beach People, chic bathing suits by Solid & Striped and designer sunglasses from Etnia Barcelona.

    Plus, our Beach House branded items such as our exclusive candles and room sprays, water bottles as well as our Endless Summer t-shirts and canvas tote bags have been super popular amongst our international and local clientele.
     
    The name the ‘Beach House Mercantile’ was inspired by the general stores of yesteryear – we loved its old world connotations and thought it was a fun fit for this one-of-a-kind retail space. It has a fresh and tasteful minimalist look, with clean lines and a palette that includes neutral colours and earth tones. 

    At our hotel, we are known for having an ‘out of the box’ approach and a very distinct aesthetic. Our philosophy is centered around what we call the Beach House Experience. Here in Cayman, we are all about enjoying the island’s endless summer. Our aim is to evoke the happy nostalgia of summer – a time when life is bright and carefree. The Beach House Mercantile is the latest addition to the fun loving lifestyle that we love sharing with our guests.

    We are offering a 20% discount for a limited time only to celebrate our Opening – make sure to swing by soon!

    The Beach House Mercantile is open daily from 9am–8pm.

    Located next to the Towel Hut and adjacent to the Turtle Lagoon.
