    WE THANK YOU FROM THE BOTTOM OF OUR HEARTS

    Here at our Beach House, we are counting our lucky stars. We have so much to be grateful for – our successful renovation, the hardworking people who helped make this transformation possible and all our guests and friends who supported us throughout the whole process. To commemorate Marriott’s Global Customer Appreciation Week, we are counting our blessings and thanking you from the bottom of our hearts.
     
    In the fast-paced and sometimes frantic world we live in, it is so easy to forget to say thank you for the simplest things, like a stranger holding open a door or picking up a dropped item. At times we feel that these gestures are to be expected and we can overlook the importance of sincere appreciation and the significance of what is often a passing but essential connection that we make with the people around us.
     
    But more than ever, it has become crucial for us to express our gratitude for even the smallest acts of kindness. A simple heartfelt thanks, expressed with authenticity, is enough for people to feel valued and special.
     
    So from all of us at the Beach House we would like to take a moment to say thank you. In honour of Marriott’s Global Customer Appreciation Week, we picked our favourite inspirational quotes on giving thanks. Thank you to all our customers for your loyalty!
     
    “The smallest act of kindness is worth more than the grandest intention.” – Kahlil Gibran

    “The essence of all beautiful art, all great art, is gratitude.” – Friedrich Nietzche

    “As we express our gratitude, we must never forget that the highest appreciation is not to utter words, but to live by them.” – John F. Kennedy

    “Gratitude is the memory of the heart.” – Jean Baptiste Massieu

    “When you are grateful – when you can see what you have – you unlock blessings to flow in your life.” – Suze Orman

    “Cultivate the habit of being grateful for every good thing that comes to you, and to give thanks continuously. And because all things have contributed to your advancement, you should include all things in your gratitude.” – Ralph Waldo Emerson

    “Those who have the ability to be grateful are the ones who have the ability to achieve greatness.” – Steve Maraboli

    “Let us be grateful to people who make us happy; they are the charming gardeners who make our souls blossom.” – Marcel Proust

    “Appreciation can make a day, even change a life. Your willingness to put it into words is all that is necessary.” – Margaret Cousins

    “Feeling gratitude and not expressing it is like wrapping a present and not giving it.” – William Arthur Ward

    “Gratitude creates the most wonderful feeling. It can resolve disputes. It can strengthen friendships. And it makes us better men and women.” – Gordon B. Hinckley
